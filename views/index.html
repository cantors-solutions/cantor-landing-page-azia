Cantor's Mission

Data-driven confidence for your next investment.

Our goal is to help all investors see which cryptocurrencies have both a stable price and healthy parameters necessary for growth. In this way, we want to reduce their potential loss, while at the same time trying to point out those assets where the increase in price is realistic to expect.

Reduce your risk

Investors should be risk averse in the crypto space. Cantor takes care that for you.

Data driven

Power your investment decisions with high volume data analysis.

Stay up to date

Cantor calculates the scores on a two hourly refresh rate.
